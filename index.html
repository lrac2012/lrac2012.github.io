Carl Laukkanen

Music Teacher & Composer

I teach Piano, Ukulele and Guitar from beginner to intermediate, young children to adults. I have taught private music lessons since 2013. Prior to teaching lessons, I worked in early childhood education and also have co-run a music / art summer camp.

My teaching is based on a foundation in music theory, technique and history. I use several different books and methods in addition to always incorporating new material. I teach from a classical foundation but equally draw upon folk, blues and popular music, as I believe it's important to expose my students to different methods, styles, and genres. This could mean working on a classical piece, drawing upon the song cannon, or incorporating the voice with playing. Eventually we might dabble in songwriting or improvisation if the student has any interest.

I also teach songwriting and improvisation specifically for the student that wants to focus on one of those. I am available for virtual lessons for students outside of the Portland area.

I teach written music as well as playing by ear. As a musician, having a strong ear is the most important asset, but I want my students to have fluidity between being on or off the page. My goal with each student is to set them up with solid fundamentals of technique, posture, and a well-tuned ear, while inspiring their curiosity, creativity and individuality. Every student is unique and I strive to help each one find their own musical path. I believe in the power and importance of music and the creative expression in the everyday.

I graduated with a BA in Music from The College of Santa Fe in NM and I have studied everything from classical, folk, jazz, improvisation, computer music, and rock. I also play the viola, kantele and drums. In my free time I have written songs and and recorded albums, as well as played in a various bands in addition to many more musical adventures.
